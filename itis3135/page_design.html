Project Overview

Application and its Purpose: Promote and facilitate the sale of handcrafted crochet items made by Hailey Smith. The items include pumpkins, octopuses, and cacti. This will be Hailey's digital storefront and portfolio, allowing visitors to see what she makes and the options they can request, see prices, and contact her for purchases or custom orders. Smith

Intended Users of the Website: Craft enthusiasts, those looking for unique gifts, crochet enjoyers, and anyone interested in handcrafted goods.

Overview of the Content of the Website: Gallery of crochet items, 'About Hailey' section, contact form, testimonials, and an FAQ section.

Wireframe & Site Map

Page Design

Home Page: Greet visitors and display some items/info. This page serves as a welcoming entrance, featuring a header with the site name & navigation, a large showcasing image, a brief introduction, a grid of featured crochet items, testimonials, and a footer.

    Audience: Website viewers.
    Interactions: Clickable page links.

Gallery Page: Showcase hand-made crochet items. This page uses a grid layout of images to exhibit the items. Each image should be clickable to view details, offering descriptions, and possibly pricing.

    Audience: Website viewers.
    Interactions: Clickable images that reveal more details on click.

About Hailey Page: Talk about who the creator is. This page includes a biography, her journey in crochet, and images of her crafting, providing a personal touch to the site.

    Audience: Website viewers.
    Interactions: Clickable images/links.

Contact Page: Display ways to contact creator. It includes a contact form with fields for name, email, subject, and message, enabling visitors to reach out for inquiries or requests.

    Audience: Website viewers.
    Actions: Submittable forms.

FAQ Page: Answer commonly asked questions. The page contains common questions about pricing, custom orders, shipping, care instructions, and more, with brief answers to each.

    Audience: Website viewers.
    Interactions: Clickable links/images.

Dynamic Functionality on the Website

Interactive Image Gallery: On the gallery page, users can click on crochet items to view larger images and details.

    Page: Gallery
    Why: To allow users to view details of each crochet item.

Contact Form: On the contact page, users can submit inquiries.

    Page: Contact
    Why: For potential customers to reach out.

FAQ Toggle: On the FAQ page, users can click on questions to reveal answers.

    Page: FAQ
    Why: To efficiently provide answers to common questions without overwhelming the visitor with text.
