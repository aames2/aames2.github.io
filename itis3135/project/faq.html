Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

To place an order, you can use the contact form on our website or email us directly at hailey@crochetcreations.com with details of the item you'd like to purchase.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments through PayPal, credit/debit card, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

Can I request a custom design?

Absolutely! We love creating custom designs. Please contact us with your ideas, and we'll work with you to make them a reality.

How should I care for my crochet items?

Each crochet item comes with its own set of care instructions. Generally, we recommend hand-washing in cold water and laying flat to dry to maintain the shape and quality.
